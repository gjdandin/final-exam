Accessibility

Accessibility is concerned with whether all users are able to access an equivalent user experience, whenever they encounter a product or service. Unlike usability, accessibility focuses on people with disabilities. Changing the mindset from “designing for me” to “designing for all” is a considerable undertaking but it will benefit both the users and the product in great deal by including every user.

One of the core values in accessibility practises is to make information available to everyone, regardless of their capabilities or situation. When we design accessibility in mind we should prioritize simplicity and perceivability in every step to ensure that it does not exclude users who have different abilities or interact with their devices in different ways.
